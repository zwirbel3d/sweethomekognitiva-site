Privacy

Sweet Home Kognitiva is a local system. There is no cloud, no external processing, and no data sharing.

Data Sources

The app accesses health data via Apple Health (HealthKit).

  • Heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Steps / activity
  • Ambient noise
  • optional sleep data

Usage

This data is used solely to make current states visible.

The app does not evaluate, predict, or pursue optimization goals.

Processing

All data is processed exclusively on the device.

There are no servers, no synchronization, and no external analysis.

Storage

Data remains on the device and is not shared with third parties.

Tracking

No tracking or analytics tools are used.

Your Control

Access to health data can be revoked at any time in the iOS settings.
